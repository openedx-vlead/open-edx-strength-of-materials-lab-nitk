OBJECTIVE:

To determine experimentally, the ultimate shear strength in double shear of mild steel plate.

STEPS:

  1. Click on the direct shear test on mild steel plate file , a window will open as shown below.

            


  2. Click on the NEXT button to move to the next step.

            


  3. Here the width and thickness of the mild steel plate is determined, then click on NEXT button to see the placing of the test set up.

            


  4. After placing the specimen in UTM move to next step by clicking on the NEXT button.

            


  5. Switch on the machine and apply load gradually.

            


  6. Load is applied till failure of the specimen, then click on NEXT button to check the results.

            


  7. The final results can be viewed from the tabular column as shown below.
